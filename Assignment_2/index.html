Regional Areas Remains Shaky

Since 2014, Northern Territory has had the highest suicide rate, peaking at 21.8 in 2014. Although, it has shown signs of decline alongside its neighbouring states (WA, TAS, QLD, SA) since 2020. Interestingly, Australian Capital Territory had halved its suicide rate to 7.7 in 2023 from its peak in 2017.

Source: Hospitalisations for intentional self-harm 2023–24 – National Hospital Morbidity Database , Australian Institute of Health & Welfare (26/08/2025).

You are not Alone.

Around the globes, it is estimated that one in eight people are affected by mental health conditions. The words in this word cloud represent the collective voice of those struggling with mental health issues in online forums and support groups. Some are associated with negative emotions such as "Depression","Anxiety", while some of the prominent words are neutral in nature. However, words such as "Love" and "Family" no matter how small, are always present in the sea of bad thoughts
A string of the words that we can weave into our own voices for those in need
