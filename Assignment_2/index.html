Regional Areas Proven Shaky

Since 2014, Northern Territory has had the highest suicide rate, peaking at 21.8 in 2014. Although, it has shown signs of decline alongside its neighbouring states (WA, TAS, QLD, SA) since 2020. Interestingly, Australian Capital Territory had halved its suicide rate to 7.7 in 2023 from its peak in 2017.
